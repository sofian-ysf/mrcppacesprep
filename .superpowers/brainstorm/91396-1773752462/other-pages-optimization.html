Section 6: Other Pages Optimization

Service pages, guide pages, and about page conversion optimization

A. Service Pages (/services/*)

Personal Statement, Experiences, Letters pages

Current: Detailed service info with basic CTAs

Add to each service page:

  • ✅ Sticky CTA bar at bottom (mobile) / side (desktop)
  • ✅ Service-specific testimonial (e.g., "My personal statement went from generic to compelling")
  • ✅ Before/after example snippet
  • ✅ "Included in all packages" badge linking to /pricing
  • ✅ FAQ section specific to that service
  • ✅ "Ready to improve your [service]?" CTA at bottom

Pill button style: All CTAs use rounded-full (pill shape) with dark background, matching preregexamprep

B. Guide Pages (/guides/*)

Personal Statement Guide, Activity Descriptions, etc.

Current: Educational content with minimal CTAs

Add contextual CTAs throughout:

  • ✅ Inline CTA boxes after key sections: "Want expert help with this? →"
  • ✅ Sidebar widget (desktop): "Get your [topic] reviewed by experts"
  • ✅ End-of-guide CTA: "Ready to apply these tips? Let our experts review your application"
  • ✅ Related service link: Guide about PS → links to /services/personal-statement
  • ✅ Success stat callout: "85% of clients who followed this advice matched"

Example inline CTA box:

Need help with your personal statement?

Our experts have reviewed 10,000+ applications

C. About Page (/about)

Strengthen credibility and add conversion path

Current: Story, mission, team, impact stats

Enhancements:

  • ✅ Lead with credentials: "Founded by physicians from [top programs]"
  • ✅ Team section with reviewer credentials (anonymized or with permission)
  • ✅ "Our reviewers include admission committee members from..." + logos
  • ✅ Impact stats prominently displayed (85% match, 10K+ reviews)
  • ✅ Mission statement focused on applicant success
  • ✅ CTA at bottom: "Ready to work with our team?" → /pricing
