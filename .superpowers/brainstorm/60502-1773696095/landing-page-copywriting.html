Key Copywriting Changes

Remove / Reduce

  • "42% didn't match" fear messaging
  • "7 seconds to impress" anxiety
  • "$300,000 lost income" scare tactics
  • Problem-focused section
  • Negative statistics emphasis

Add / Emphasize

  • "85% match rate" confidence
  • "2× the national average" proof
  • Reviewer credentials upfront
  • "Match at your dream program" aspiration
  • Live activity indicators
  • Specific program names where clients matched
