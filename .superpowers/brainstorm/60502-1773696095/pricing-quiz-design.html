Quiz Scoring Logic

Rule: Quiz always recommends Premium or Complete (never Comprehensive)


• Any "Complete" trigger → Recommend Complete ($999)

- Timeline < 2 weeks

- Competitive specialty

- Reapplicant


• Otherwise → Recommend Premium ($499)


Result: ~70% of users see Complete recommended, ~30% see Premium
