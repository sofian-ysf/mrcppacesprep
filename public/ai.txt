## MRCP PACES Exam Information
Exam Type: Clinical skills assessment
Format: 5 stations with real patients and simulated scenarios
Duration: Approximately 90 minutes total
Stations:
- Station 1: Respiratory and Abdominal examination
- Station 2: History taking
- Station 3: Cardiovascular and Neurological examination
- Station 4: Communication skills and Ethics
- Station 5: Brief clinical consultations (2 encounters)
Governing Body: Royal College of Physicians (MRCP UK)
Retake Wait Period: 3 months between attempts
